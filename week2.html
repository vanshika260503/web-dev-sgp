Welcome to Nitk beach

NITK Beach is a private beach situated on the south-western seaboard of the Indian sub-continent, adjacent to Suratkhal at Mangalore in Karnataka. It is 20 km from the centre of Mangalore city. It was later named after the nearby NITK (National Institute of Technology, Karnataka). It is a private beach. A lighthouse constructed in the year 1972 is very near to this beach
